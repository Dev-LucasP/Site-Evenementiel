Who are we?

LenExpo is a website dedicated to offering pop culture-themed conventions.

We are a company located in the north of France whose goal is to organize pop culture events available to everyone.

The company was created by 3 people:

Gomez Gonzalez Enzo

I've had a passion for Pop-Culture since I was very young. I started collecting figurines and goodies in 2008 and haven't stopped since. As an early fan, I felt it was my duty to bring other fans together around a common passion. That's why my two partners and I created Len Expo.

Perez Lucas

As a fan of pop culture from an early age, I wanted to bring people together so that they could immerse themselves in the excitement of a captivating convention and have wonderful memories with their loved ones.

Pierreuse Nathan

As a lifelong fan of pop culture, I decided to take part in this project to bring together all the people who share this passion so that everyone can have a great time at our various conventions.

What is our company doing?

The LEN expo company was founded by the above 3 people, and has been running conventions in several locations in France, with plans to expand to other countries to share this passion for pop culture with even more people!

Our head office is in Lens, where we plan and organize our next events with the help of our collaborators and employees.

The LEN expo project began at the start of 2022, during which we forged links with collaborators and found the material to achieve our goal of organizing our first convention.

LEN expo is currently focused on pop culture events, but in the future we may also organize events on other subjects.

LEN expo is still a new company on the market, and we're hoping to stand out from the crowd, as the competition is tough. Despite this, we've managed to bring together a host of exhibitors and guests for this first edition, some of whom you can find on our home page.

Follow us on our social networks so you don't miss a thing!
